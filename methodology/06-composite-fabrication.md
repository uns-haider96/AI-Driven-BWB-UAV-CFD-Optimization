# Composite Fabrication and Structural Validation

The optimized blended-wing-body UAV geometry was translated from CAD and CFD optimization into a physical prototype through a mold-based composite manufacturing workflow.

## Manufacturing Workflow

1. **CAD-to-mold translation**  
   The optimized UAV surface was used to generate matched mold geometry suitable for composite layup.

2. **3D printed mold tooling**  
   The mold was manufactured in segmented parts using additive manufacturing. The images below show mold production and assembled mold sections.

3. **Mold preparation**  
   Mold surfaces were sanded, cleaned, aligned, and prepared for composite processing.

4. **Carbon-fiber/epoxy layup**  
   The airframe was manufactured using carbon-fiber/epoxy composite material. The visible woven composite surface confirms that the project advanced beyond simulation into real prototype fabrication.

5. **Vacuum-assisted fabrication**  
   The composite layup was prepared for vacuum-assisted processing to improve surface conformity and laminate consolidation.

6. **Demolding and finishing**  
   The prototype was removed from the mold, trimmed, and prepared for testing.

## Structural Validation Scope

The prototype manufacturing work was supported by structural feasibility assessment. The airframe concept used carbon-fiber/epoxy composite construction to balance stiffness, strength, and weight.

The structural assessment supported the manufacturability and feasibility of the optimized BWB UAV geometry rather than treating the project as a simulation-only study.

## Technical Significance

This manufacturing stage shows that the research was not only a numerical CFD/surrogate-modeling exercise. The optimized BWB surface was converted into a manufacturable airframe through physical tooling and composite processing.

This strengthens the engineering value of the project by connecting aerodynamic optimization, CAD surface generation, mold tooling, composite fabrication, prototype realization, and experimental wind tunnel testing.
