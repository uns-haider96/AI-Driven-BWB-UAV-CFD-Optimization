## Public Interpretation

These results should be presented as evidence of physical testing and experimental aerodynamic characterization. The results are useful because they show that the UAV was not only designed and simulated, but also manufactured and placed in a wind tunnel test environment.

## Recommended Wording

> A manufactured carbon-fiber/epoxy BWB UAV prototype was tested in a wind tunnel to observe lift, drag, side force, and moment behavior across multiple speed/angle cases. The experiment supports prototype-level aerodynamic evaluation and links the CFD optimization workflow with physical testing.

## Do Not Overclaim

Avoid saying:

- “The CFD was perfectly validated.”
- “The wind tunnel fully proves all simulation values.”
- “The prototype achieved exact predicted L/D.”

A more credible statement is:

> Wind tunnel testing provided experimental support and trend-level aerodynamic characterization of the manufactured prototype.
