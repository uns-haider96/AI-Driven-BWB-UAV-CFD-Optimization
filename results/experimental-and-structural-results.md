# Experimental and Structural Results

## Overview

The project progressed beyond numerical simulation into physical prototype manufacturing and wind tunnel testing. This section summarizes the experimental and structural significance of that work.

## Structural Assessment

The optimized UAV was evaluated for structural feasibility using composite design considerations and FEA-informed assessment. The airframe concept used carbon-fiber/epoxy composite construction to balance stiffness, strength, manufacturability, and weight.

The structural work supports the feasibility of the optimized design under expected UAV-scale loading conditions.

## Interpretation

The prototype manufacturing and wind tunnel testing significantly strengthen the project because they demonstrate a complete engineering workflow:

```text
Design → CFD → Optimization → Manufacturing → Prototype → Wind Tunnel Testing
```

The wind tunnel results should be interpreted as prototype-level experimental characterization rather than perfect one-to-one CFD validation.
